    Pixel‑to‑Hex — C‑array mach chenmoH (0xFF rur). Pixel‑to‑Binary — binary mIllogh chenmoH (0b10101010 rur). Pixel‑to‑ASCII — ASCII mIllogh chenmoH.

    Mini‑mIllogh (1:1) leQHa’meH. row/column toggle wa’ clickDaq. ngoqHom nItlh ngoqlIj chenmoHlu' 'ej DaqDaq vay' lanlaH. template & stamp pat lo’qa’meH. De’ qach wa’DIch — nap, nIb, Qapchu’.
